Mold Maintenance Staff

location_on Cabuyao, Laguna

Full-Time Permanent Open 8 hour shift 10 hour shift Production Staff Mid-Career

Responsible for mold assembly, repair, maintenance, and ensuring operational standards and safety in the workplace.

  • Male or Female
  • Candidate must have at least a Vocational Degree equivalent to Industrial and Mechanical Engineering
  • Knowledgeable in Injection/Mold parameter setup.
  • Willing to work in Cabuyao, Laguna.
  • Willing to work shifting and overtime schedule setup

  • Performs mold assembly and disassembly as scheduled
  • Performs needed repair of Molds as per Mold request
  • Performs required processes for repair
  • Responsible for the safekeeping of mold (preparation and storage).

  • Implements the use of PPE's to promote safety and Health within the area of responsibility.
