Privacy Policy

Gunma Gohkin Co., Ltd. (hereinafter referred to as "our company") treats personal information in accordance with the Personal Information Protection Law and other related laws and regulations and handles the following in order to protect customer's personal information.

Provision of Personal Information

Our company observes the restricted policy on providing personal information on customer's whereabouts. All personal information will be made available only upon the customer's consent.

Purposes of Using Personal Information

  1. To provide customers with our product information, company information, services, and other related information;
  2. To respond to the inquiries of customers;
  3. To contact the customer for necessary information;
  4. To provide information related to recruitment and application, and to respond to their inquiries;
  5. Other purposes for use based on laws and regulations;

In order to acquire/use personal information for purposes not stipulated above, customer's consent is necessary in advance.

Use and Disclosure of Personal Information

All personal information provided shall only be used for its intended purpose only. Also, personal information provided shall only be shared within the range of purpose of utilization between our company and its subsidiaries and/or affiliates, the responsible department is Gunma Gohkin Co., Ltd. General Affairs Department.

Our company will not disclose any personal information provided by customers to third parties without the customer's consent or other justifiable reason based on laws and regulations.

Personal Information Management

Our company will properly manage the personal information provided by the customer and make efforts for safety measures against improper access, divulgence, loss, destruction, falsification, and misuse of personal information.

Others

In compliance with the rules and regulations on information technology, modification and revision of the contents of this website is possible, especially if it is for improvement purposes.

Our company is not, however, responsible for the protection of personal information on other websites if it is linked to this website.

Please check these policies before providing your personal information.
