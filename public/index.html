Welcome to Gunma Gohkin Philippines Corporation

Casting Excellence, Moulding the Future

Since its establishment as a pioneer in die-casting technology, the Gunma Gohkin Group has been delivering high-quality automotive parts to both local and global markets. In 1995, the growing demand paved the way for the founding of Gunma Gohkin Philippines Corporation, marking a new chapter of growth and partnership in the region.

Guided by the values of Technology, Knowledge, and Passion, GGPC continues to advance toward a sustainable and smarter future. United as one family, we remain committed to excellence, innovation, and trust—striving to achieve 100 Years of Excellent Service as the Preferred Supplier of our customers.

Milestone Event September 30, 2025

Gunma Gohkin Philippines Celebrates 30 Years of Excellence

This year marks a significant milestone for Gunma Gohkin Philippines Corporation as we celebrate our 30th Anniversary — three remarkable decades of excellence, unwavering teamwork, and consistent growth.

To commemorate this achievement, we held a special Family Day for our valued employees and dedicated partners last September 13, 2025, at Villa Leonila Events Place. The event was a day filled with joy and unity, celebrating our shared journey.

During the celebration, we took the opportunity to honor the invaluable contributions of our incredible team. Their dedication, passion, and hard work have been instrumental in shaping Gunma Gohkin Philippines into the successful company it is today.
