Precision in Every Part

Gunma Gohkin utilizes advanced die-casting technology to produce high-precision aluminum parts that meet the stringent requirements of various industries. Our commitment to innovation ensures top-quality products for our global clientele.

Automobile-Related Parts

We specialize in manufacturing critical components for the automotive industry, ensuring reliability and performance. Our parts are integral to engine, transmission, and power steering systems.

A crucial housing that protects the starter motor's pinion gear, ensuring reliable engine starts.

Housing for Power Steering

Robust and lightweight housing for power steering systems, enhancing vehicle maneuverability.

Transmission Parts

Quality and Excellence at Our Core

Our two state-of-the-art facilities in the Philippines are the backbone of our production, each specializing in different areas but united by a single quality policy.

Laguna Plant

Focusing on high-volume production of automobile-related parts, the Laguna Plant is equipped with state-of-the-art die-casting machines and automated production lines to meet the just-in-time demands of our automotive clients.
