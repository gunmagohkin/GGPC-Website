Message

"Since its establishment as a pioneer in die-casting technology, Gunma Gohkin Group has been providing transport vehicle parts for the local and global markets. In 1995, the demand brought about by the prevailing market conditions became an opportunity for Gunma Gohkin Co., Ltd. to establish its overseas subsidiary in the Philippines. While in 2010, "Technical Center" was established to pursue a technology-driven company. In 2011, a carbon dioxide reduction project was launched to uphold its commitment to the environment. Also, a group-wide company activity on Compact-Total Productivity Management/Maintenance (C-TPM), was launched and supported by all its employees, which has provided them the opportunity to become UNITED for continuous improvement. Now, the company focuses on three areas, namely, TECHNOLOGY, KNOWLEDGE, and PASSION, to achieve its 100th Year of high-quality service."

Junichi Roponggi
