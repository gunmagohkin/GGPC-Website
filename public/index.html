Welcome to Gunma Gohkin Philippines Corporation

Casting Excellence, Moulding the Future

Since its establishment as a pioneer in die-casting technology, the Gunma Gohkin Group has been delivering high-quality automotive parts to both local and global markets. In 1995, the growing demand paved the way for the founding of Gunma Gohkin Philippines Corporation, marking a new chapter of growth and partnership in the region.

Guided by the values of Technology, Knowledge, and Passion, GGPC continues to advance toward a sustainable and smarter future. United as one family, we remain committed to excellence, innovation, and trust—striving to achieve 100 Years of Excellent Service as the Preferred Supplier of our customers.

Milestone Event September 30, 2025

Gunma Gohkin Philippines Celebrates 30 Years of Excellence

This year marks a significant milestone for Gunma Gohkin Philippines Corporation as we celebrate our 30th Anniversary — three remarkable decades of excellence, unwavering teamwork, and consistent growth.

To commemorate this achievement, we held a special Family Day for our valued employees and dedicated partners last September 13, 2025, at Villa Leonila Events Place. The event was a day filled with joy and unity, celebrating our shared journey. As we reflected on our history, the celebration was not just about looking back, but also about strengthening our culture for the future. The festivities included team activities and shared meals that symbolized our gratitude for the past 30 years and our excitement for the decades to come.

United as One Family

Chairman’s Birthday & Family Day

The day was a truly meaningful celebration — a moment to honor the past, appreciate the present, and look forward to the bright future of Gunma Gohkin. It was a time to reflect on our incredible journey together, recognizing the unwavering dedication and hard work of every individual.

Reconnecting with colleagues, both past and present, reinforced the sense of community and shared purpose that makes GGPC such a special place to belong to.

"As we look ahead, we do so with renewed optimism and determination. With the passion, talent, and unity of our people, we are ready to write the next chapter of GGPC's story."

Kaizen Recognition & SDG Kickoff

During the program, Kaizen Awardees were recognized for their outstanding contributions to process improvement. Their projects demonstrated creative solutions that enhanced productivity, safety, and operational efficiency across various departments.

This event highlights not only our commitment to the UN SDGs but also our dedication to fostering a culture of Kaizen. Our SDG initiatives are thoughtfully crafted to address key global challenges, reducing our environmental footprint, optimizing resource efficiency, and upholding ethical practices.

Exhibition August 28-31, 2024

GGPC Showcases Innovation at PDMEX 2024

Introduction

Gunma Gohkin Philippines Corp. (GGPC) proudly participated in the 11th edition of the Philippine Die & Mould Machineries and Equipment Exhibition (PDMEX) held at the World Trade Center in Pasay City, Manila. As an esteemed PEZA Pavilion member, GGPC showcased its latest innovations and capabilities. This opportunity allowed GGPC to demonstrate its commitment to excellence in the die and mould industry.

GGPC Booth #380 at PEZA Pavilion

At Booth 380, GGPC highlighted one of its flagship products—high-precision cast components known for their exceptional durability and performance. These components have been engineered to withstand the rigorous demands of various industrial applications. By presenting these innovations, GGPC demonstrated its technical capacity and reinforced its position as a leader in the manufacturing sector.

Team Force & Exhibitors

GGPC's representatives actively engaged with fellow exhibitors, industry experts, and potential clients. Additionally, GGPC highlighted its commitment to sustainability by discussing eco-friendly manufacturing practices and energy-efficient technologies. These interactions helped identify new business opportunities and potential partnerships.

INGRESS PHOTOS & FEEDBACK (Aug 27, 2024)

The Ingress phase sets the stage for the exhibition. It's when all the behind-the-scenes action happens, with exhibitors coming together to get their booths right. This period is all about careful planning and making sure everything looks perfect for when the visitors arrive. The hard work during Ingress shows just how committed everyone is to putting their best foot forward.

DAY 1 PHOTOS & FEEDBACK (Aug 28, 2024)

Officially, PDMEX 2024 opened with a grand opening ceremony featuring speeches from industry leaders and government officials. Attendees were treated to a showcase of cutting-edge technologies and innovations in the die and mould sector. Exhibitors, including GGPC, felt the excitement as they showcased their latest products to their audience.

DAY 2 PHOTOS & FEEDBACK (Aug 29, 2024)

On the second day, many visitors, including students and professionals, filled the exhibition halls. Students were especially excited to see the latest technologies firsthand. Exhibitors enjoyed sharing insights about new trends in the die and mould industry. The lively mix of curious students and experienced professionals created an energetic atmosphere.

DAY 3 PHOTOS & FEEDBACK (Aug 30, 2024)

Just like the second day, the third day stayed busy. It was clear that PDMEX is not just about business, but about building connections and exchanging ideas. In the exhibition hall, interactive displays allowed students to get hands-on experience with cutting-edge tools. Many companies offered live demonstrations which captivated the audience.

EGRESS PHOTOS & FEEDBACK

After the final day as attendees exited, it signified the start of fresh possibilities. The dedication shown by all attendees has raised expectations for upcoming events, with everyone excited to see what PDMEX 2026 has in store. With a common goal of advancing, the community is prepared to move forward.

Company Events May 7, 2025

GGPC Joins LISP1 Inter-Locators Sportsfest 2025

Introduction

GGPC is excited to take part in the LISP1 Inter-Locators Sportsfest 2025. Our company is excited to showcase its athletic abilities and foster a strong sense of teamwork with fellow participants in this eagerly awaited annual event. This marks our return after a 17-year hiatus since our last participation.

Known for both innovation and great team spirit, GGPC's involvement is sure to bring extra energy and excitement to the Sportsfest. Employees from all departments are preparing to compete in various sports, demonstrating not only their physical abilities but also the cooperative spirit that represents GGPC.

The Opening Day

The LISP1 Inter-Locators Sportsfest 2025 kicked off with an energetic opening ceremony held at the LISP1 Gym on May 25, 2025. GGPC proudly took part in the opening parade, led by Ms. Andrea Bertos, the muse, and Mr. Mark Camarillo. Demonstrating strong company support, Mr. Takanobu Nakajima, GGPC's Vice President, also actively participated in the parade.

Additionally, many GGPC employees attended the opening day to show their solidarity and cheer on their colleagues competing in the event. Their presence created a lively and supportive atmosphere, boosting the morale of the athletes and reinforcing the company's strong sense of community.

The players, along with GGPC's super-supportive crew.

Ms. Andrea Bertos, Mr. Takanobu Nakajima, and Mr. Mark Camarillo.

Search for Best Muse 2025

Adding another layer of excitement and anticipation to the day, the very smart and beautiful Ms. Andrea Bertos also graced the stage as a participant in the Best Muse competition. Her performance was truly captivating, as she exuded charm, poise, and genuine enthusiasm, delighting the appreciative audience.

While the coveted title of Best Muse 2025 was ultimately awarded to the deserving representative from PRICON Microelectronics Inc., Ms. Bertos' remarkable performance and radiant presence undeniably contributed significantly to the joyful and communal atmosphere of the LISP1 Inter-Locators Sportsfest. Her spirited participation was a wonderful highlight of the opening day, embodying the true spirit of friendly competition and camaraderie that the event aimed to celebrate.
